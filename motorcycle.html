Learning to Ride

Harley Sportster

My family has been Harley owners since before I was born. From my uncles, to my cousins, my brother, and even my mom, my whole family has always owned and rode Harleys. My mom learned how to ride so her and my dad could ride separately. This was a huge goal for her. I was so proud when she passed her test and then bought her own Harley. This has inspired me to want to join in on the fun and learn to ride so we can ride as a family. My goal is to own a Harley Sportster. They are my favorite kind of Harley.
